ABOUT THIS PROJECT

What is StellarGaze?
StellarGaze is an innovative web app that brings you closer to the stars by offering 3D views of celestial bodies as seen from exoplanets orbiting distant stars. It's a powerful tool for both learning and exploration, offering detailed visuals and interactive features for space enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Why Explore the Universe from Exoplanets?
Earth's view of the universe is just one perspective. StellarGaze opens up the stars from entirely new locations, letting you see how constellations and stars shift across the galaxy. Travel beyond the solar system and experience the beauty of the cosmos from alien worlds.
